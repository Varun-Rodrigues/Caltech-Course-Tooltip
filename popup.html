User Guide

Basic Usage

Hover over highlighted course codes on any webpage to see a tooltip with course information. Click on a course code to pin the tooltip for detailed reading.

Pinned Tooltips

When a tooltip is pinned, you can scroll through courses with long descriptions. Click outside the tooltip or press ESC to unpin.

Course Ranges & Cycling

For course ranges like "SEC 11-13" or multi-section courses, click repeatedly on the highlighted text to cycle through each individual course.

Course Search

Use the course lookup above to search for specific courses directly. This is especially useful when viewing PDFs or documents where automatic detection may not work.

Customization

Configure the settings below to customize what information appears in the tooltips, such as prerequisites, descriptions, and instructor details.
